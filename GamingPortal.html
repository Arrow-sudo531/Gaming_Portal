NEON STRIKE V2 // PILOT BRIEFING

Mission Objective

You are the pilot of a neon strike craft positioned at the bottom of the screen. Your task: eliminate falling enemy units and survive as long as possible. Destroy enemies to increase your score and avoid letting them collide with your ship or escape past you.

Controls

  • ← Arrow Left – Move ship left
  • → Arrow Right – Move ship right
  • Spacebar – Fire neon projectile upwards

Core Mechanics

  • Your ship is represented by a triangular neon craft near the bottom of the playfield.
  • Enemies spawn from the top and drift downward, sometimes with slight horizontal movement.
  • Each enemy destroyed triggers a neon explosion and rewards you with +10 score.
  • If an enemy collides with your ship or passes the bottom edge, you lose 1 life.

Health & Lives

  • You start with 3 lives (displayed as a health bar in the top-right HUD).
  • Every hit or missed enemy reduces your health.
  • When lives reach zero: GAME OVER is triggered and your final score is displayed.

Damage & Invincibility

  • When you take damage, your ship enters a short invincibility window and flashes to indicate a hit.
  • During this period, further collisions will not remove additional lives.

How to Start / Restart

  • When the game loads, an overlay shows PRESS START and a START button.
  • Click the START button to begin the game loop.
  • After game over, the overlay shows your FINAL SCORE and a RESTART button to play again.

Recommended Environment

  • Play on a desktop/laptop with a keyboard for best experience.
  • Ensure the browser tab is focused so keyboard controls (arrows + space) are captured correctly.
