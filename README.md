## ‚öôÔ∏è Game Rules & Mechanics

### 1. Enemy Spawning  
Enemies continuously spawn from the top of the screen and travel downward with slight horizontal movement.

### 2. Shooting System  
The player fires neon projectiles upward.  
- Each successful hit destroys an enemy  
- Each hit awards **+10 points**

### 3. Collision System  
The player loses **one life** if:
- An enemy collides with the ship  
- An enemy crosses the bottom of the screen  

### 4. Health & Lives  
- Player starts with **3 lives**
- A real-time **health bar** is displayed on the top-right
- When lives reach **zero**, the game immediately ends

### 5. Invincibility System  
After taking damage:
- The player becomes **temporarily invincible**
- A flashing effect indicates invincibility

### 6. Particle Effects  
Neon particle explosions appear when:
- An enemy is destroyed
- The player takes damage
